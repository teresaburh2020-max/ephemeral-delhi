💡 Core Idea

This project documents and analyzes temporary structures in Delhi—spaces that appear, transform the city for a short time, and then disappear.

Built quickly
Highly symbolic
Deeply cultural
Rarely archived

The project treats them as cultural texts rather than just physical structures.

🎯 Research Questions

01

How does temporary architecture reflect Delhi's cultural, religious, and political life?

02

What meanings are embedded in design, color, and materials?

03

How do these structures reshape public space temporarily?

04

What happens to cultural memory once these structures are dismantled?
